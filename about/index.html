About Me

Hi! My name is Konstantinos Pouchias.I am a Mechanical Engineer with a specialization in Industrial Management, pursuing my goal to become a Data Scientist.

About Me

My love for science and my desire to solve problems led me to choose mechanical engineering as my field studies. My studies in Engineering helped me to further sharpen my problem-solving skills and ultimately taught me a way to approach and deal with any challenges that may arise in my life.

During my studies, I became passionate about applied mathematics, statistics, mathematical optimization, and programming. My greatest achievement by far is my thesis project. It concerned the implementation of an optimization algorithm, using C programming language, for optimal price bidding in electricity markets by energy producers.

In addition to my education, I recently completed the IBM Data Science Professional Certificate by Coursera. During that time I learned the fundamentals of data science methodology and I developed hands-on skills in Data Science and Machine Learning. I used a variety of data science tools and learned Python and SQL. I performed Data Visualization, Data Analysis and I created Machine Learning Models. Finally, I had to apply and demonstrate my skills and knowledge in a Capstone project.

My background in engineering has helped me become more analytical and detail-oriented. I am a highly ambitious person that strives to be the best in all he does. I am very resilient and I believe that hard work is the key to achieving your goals.

Currently, I am seeking for my opportunities to help me start my career as a Data Scientist.
